Current Research Projects

Our lab investigates several core areas of allergic inflammation and immune dysfunction.

IgE Signaling in Mast Cells

Studying the downstream effects of IgE cross-linking on mast cell activation.

Allergic Asthma Models

Utilizing murine models to examine airway remodeling in chronic allergy.

Precision Allergy Diagnostics

Developing point-of-care tools for rapid allergy screening using engineered biosensors.
