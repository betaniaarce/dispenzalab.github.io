Pioneering Allergy Research

About the Lab

The Dispenza Lab is dedicated to advancing our understanding of allergic disease mechanisms and improving therapeutic outcomes for patients. We investigate immune responses related to IgE-mediated mast cell activation, develop novel allergy interventions, and map environmental allergen patterns. Our interdisciplinary team combines immunology, bioengineering, and translational science to address the most pressing challenges in allergy research.
