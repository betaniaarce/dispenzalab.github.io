Pioneering Allergy Research

About Our Lab

The Dispenza Lab is focused on understanding the molecular and cellular mechanisms underlying allergic inflammation, with a mission to improve patient outcomes through translational research.

Research Focus

We study the biology of mast cells, basophils, and IgE-mediated pathways in allergy and asthma.

Team Collaboration

Our multidisciplinary team works across immunology, biomedical engineering, and clinical science.

Translational Impact

We strive to translate bench research into novel diagnostics and therapies.
