Innovating Allergy Science

Exploring immune responses and pioneering solutions to allergic diseases.

About Our Lab

Our lab is dedicated to advancing the understanding of allergic mechanisms, immune responses, and environmental triggers. We aim to develop therapies and interventions to improve the quality of life for allergy sufferers worldwide.

Cutting-Edge Research

We use molecular and computational techniques to study IgE responses and mast cell activation.

Multidisciplinary Team

Our team includes immunologists, engineers, clinicians, and data scientists.

Global Impact

We collaborate with institutions worldwide to tackle allergy challenges at scale.
